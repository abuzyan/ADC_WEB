Communication Systems & Signal Processing Academy A comprehensive, interactive web-based reference for Signals & Systems, Analog Communication, and Digital Communication. Designed as a complete guide for engineering students and professionals to master core ECE concepts.

🚀 Live Demo Check out the live website here: https://abuzyan.github.io/ADC_WEBSITE_/

📖 Features

Signals & Systems Classification: Detailed breakdown of Continuous-time vs. Discrete-time, Deterministic vs. Random, and Periodic vs. Non-periodic signals.
Core Concepts: In-depth coverage of LTI systems, Convolution, Fourier Analysis, and Sampling theory.

Mathematical Precision: All formulas are rendered clearly for quick reference.

Analog Communication Modulation Techniques: Complete guides on Amplitude Modulation (AM), Frequency Modulation (FM), and Phase Modulation (PM).
Noise Performance: Analysis of SNR and the impact of noise on various modulation schemes.

Digital Communication Modulation Schemes: Covers ASK, FSK, PSK, and QAM.
Advanced Topics: Insights into Channel Coding, Error Correction, and Multiple Access techniques.

Comparative Analysis Side-by-side comparisons (e.g., AM vs. FM vs. PM and Analog vs. Digital) to help understand trade-offs in engineering design.
🛠️ Built With HTML5/CSS3: For a responsive, modern "Dark Mode" UI.

JavaScript: Powers the interactive navigation and sidebar logic.

GitHub Pages: For seamless hosting and deployment.
